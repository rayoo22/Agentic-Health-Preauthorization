# POLICY DOCUMENT - POL004
## Senior Care Plan

### Policy Information
- **Policy ID:** POL004
- **Policy Name:** Senior Care Plan
- **Policy Type:** Senior (Age 60+)
- **Effective Date:** January 1, 2024
- **Expiry Date:** December 31, 2024

### Coverage Summary
- **Annual Coverage Limit:** $100,000.00
- **Annual Deductible:** $750.00
- **Copay Percentage:** 15%

### Covered Services
1. Chronic disease management
2. Regular health screenings
3. Prescription medications
4. Outpatient consultations
5. Emergency care
6. Diagnostic tests
7. Physical therapy

### Exclusions
1. Cosmetic procedures
2. Experimental drugs
3. Elective surgeries
4. Dental care

### Terms and Conditions
- Designed specifically for members 60 years and older
- Covers chronic conditions including diabetes, hypertension, arthritis
- Pre-authorization required for hospitalization
- Includes wellness programs and preventive care
- After deductible, member pays 15% copay on covered services
- Maximum out-of-pocket limit: $8,000 per year
- Free annual health assessment included

### Claims Process
1. Pre-authorization required for hospitalization
2. Chronic disease management requires care plan enrollment
3. Submit claim within 30 days with required documentation
4. Claims processed within 10 business days
5. Direct payment to providers
