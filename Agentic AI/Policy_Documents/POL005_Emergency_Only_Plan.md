# POLICY DOCUMENT - POL005
## Emergency Only Plan

### Policy Information
- **Policy ID:** POL005
- **Policy Name:** Emergency Only Plan
- **Policy Type:** Individual
- **Effective Date:** January 1, 2024
- **Expiry Date:** December 31, 2024

### Coverage Summary
- **Annual Coverage Limit:** $25,000.00
- **Annual Deductible:** $2,000.00
- **Copay Percentage:** 30%

### Covered Services
1. Emergency room visits
2. Urgent care
3. Accident-related treatments
4. Emergency surgeries
5. Ambulance services

### Exclusions
1. Routine checkups
2. Preventive care
3. Elective procedures
4. Prescription medications (non-emergency)
5. Chronic disease management

### Terms and Conditions
- Coverage limited to emergency and urgent care only
- Must be treated within 24 hours of incident
- No coverage for follow-up care beyond initial emergency treatment
- After deductible, member pays 30% copay on covered services
- Maximum out-of-pocket limit: $12,000 per year
- Emergency defined as life-threatening or severe injury requiring immediate care

### Claims Process
1. No pre-authorization required for emergency services
2. Treatment must occur within 24 hours of emergency incident
3. Submit claim within 15 days with emergency documentation
4. Claims processed within 7 business days
5. Member responsible for follow-up care costs

### Contact Information
- Claims Department: emergency@healthplan.com
- Phone: 1-800-EMERG-1
- Hours: 24/7 Emergency Line
