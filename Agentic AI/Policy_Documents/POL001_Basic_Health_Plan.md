# POLICY DOCUMENT - POL001
## Basic Health Plan

### Policy Information
- **Policy ID:** POL001
- **Policy Name:** Basic Health Plan
- **Policy Type:** Individual
- **Effective Date:** January 1, 2024
- **Expiry Date:** December 31, 2024

### Coverage Summary
- **Annual Coverage Limit:** $50,000.00
- **Annual Deductible:** $1,000.00
- **Copay Percentage:** 20%

### Covered Services
1. Outpatient consultations
2. Laboratory tests
3. X-rays
4. Prescription medications
5. Emergency care
6. Preventive care

### Exclusions
1. Cosmetic procedures
2. Experimental treatments
3. Pre-existing conditions (first 6 months)

### Terms and Conditions
- Coverage requires pre-authorization for procedures over $5,000
- Claims must be submitted within 30 days of service
- Annual limit applies per calendar year
- Member must pay deductible before coverage begins
- After deductible, member pays 20% copay on covered services
- Maximum out-of-pocket limit: $10,000 per year

### Claims Process
1. Obtain pre-authorization for procedures over $5,000
2. Receive medical service from network provider
3. Submit claim within 30 days with required documentation
4. Claims processed within 15 business days
5. Payment issued to provider or member as applicable

### Contact Information
- Claims Department: claims@healthplan.com
- Phone: 1-800-HEALTH-1
- Hours: Monday-Friday, 8 AM - 6 PM
