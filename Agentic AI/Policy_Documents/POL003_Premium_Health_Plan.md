# POLICY DOCUMENT - POL003
## Premium Health Plan

### Policy Information
- **Policy ID:** POL003
- **Policy Name:** Premium Health Plan
- **Policy Type:** Individual
- **Effective Date:** January 1, 2024
- **Expiry Date:** December 31, 2024

### Coverage Summary
- **Annual Coverage Limit:** $250,000.00
- **Annual Deductible:** $250.00
- **Copay Percentage:** 5%

### Covered Services
1. All inpatient and outpatient services
2. Surgical procedures
3. Advanced diagnostics (MRI, CT scans)
4. Specialist consultations
5. Prescription medications
6. Rehabilitation services
7. Home healthcare
8. Ambulance services

### Exclusions
1. Experimental treatments
2. Cosmetic procedures (non-reconstructive)
3. Long-term care facilities

### Terms and Conditions
- No referrals required for specialists
- Pre-authorization needed only for procedures exceeding $10,000
- Includes international emergency coverage
- After deductible, member pays only 5% copay on covered services
- Maximum out-of-pocket limit: $5,000 per year
- 24/7 concierge medical support included
- Access to premium network of providers

### Claims Process
1. Pre-authorization only for procedures over $10,000
2. Direct billing to insurance for network providers
3. Submit claim within 60 days for out-of-network services
4. Claims processed within 5 business days
5. Expedited reimbursement available

- Claims Department: premium@healthplan.com

- Hours: 24/7 Support
