# POLICY DOCUMENT - POL002
## Comprehensive Health Plan

### Policy Information
- **Policy ID:** POL002
- **Policy Name:** Comprehensive Health Plan
- **Policy Type:** Family
- **Effective Date:** January 1, 2024
- **Expiry Date:** December 31, 2024

### Coverage Summary
- **Annual Coverage Limit:** $150,000.00
- **Annual Deductible:** $500.00
- **Copay Percentage:** 10%

### Covered Services
1. Inpatient hospitalization
2. Surgical procedures
3. Outpatient care
4. Laboratory and diagnostic tests
5. Prescription drugs
6. Maternity care
7. Mental health services
8. Physical therapy

### Exclusions
1. Cosmetic surgery
2. Dental procedures (except accident-related)
3. Vision correction surgery
4. Alternative medicine

### Terms and Conditions
- Pre-authorization required for elective surgeries and hospitalization
- Maximum 30 days inpatient coverage per year
- Specialist referrals required from primary care physician
- Family coverage includes spouse and up to 4 dependent children
- After deductible, member pays 10% copay on covered services
- Maximum out-of-pocket limit: $15,000 per family per year

### Claims Process
1. Obtain referral from primary care physician for specialists
2. Get pre-authorization for elective surgeries and hospitalization
3. Submit claim within 30 days with required documentation
4. Claims processed within 10 business days
5. Direct payment to network providers
